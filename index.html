Our Mission

Safe Spaces Foundation is dedicated to democratizing access to mental health care across India. We believe that quality mental wellness support should not be a privilege reserved for the few, but a fundamental right accessible to all.

Through innovative technology, culturally-sensitive approaches, and strategic partnerships, we're bridging the mental health gap in rural communities, underserved schools, small businesses, and marginalized populations.

As the charitable arm of My Safe Spaces, we leverage cutting-edge AI technology and evidence-based practices to provide free or subsidized mental health resources where they're needed most.

Where We Focus

Rural Communities

Bringing mental health awareness and support to India's villages where traditional stigma and lack of access create significant barriers to care.

Underserved Schools

Providing free mental wellness programs to government schools and institutions that lack resources for student counseling and support.

Small Businesses

Offering subsidized workplace wellness programs to small and medium enterprises that cannot afford comprehensive employee mental health benefits.

Marginalized Groups

Serving LGBTQ+ communities, women facing domestic challenges, and other vulnerable populations with culturally-competent, trauma-informed care.

Professional Training

Providing scholarships and reduced-cost PRISM certification to aspiring mental health professionals from underrepresented backgrounds.

Crisis Intervention

Operating 24/7 crisis support services and connecting individuals in acute distress with immediate professional help.

Our Impact

Free Access Programs

Providing completely free IntelliCare AI support and Journi journaling tools to verified underserved communities and individuals in need.

School Partnerships

Sponsoring My Safe Schools implementation in government and low-income private schools, reaching thousands of students annually.

Training Scholarships

Offering full and partial scholarships for PRISM professional certification to candidates from rural areas and marginalized backgrounds.

Community Outreach

Conducting mental health awareness campaigns, workshops, and screenings in underserved communities across India.

Research & Advocacy

Supporting research on mental health disparities and advocating for policy changes that improve mental health access nationwide.

Technology Innovation

Developing and maintaining AI-powered tools optimized for low-bandwidth areas and multiple Indian languages to maximize accessibility.
